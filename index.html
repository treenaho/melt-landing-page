Questions, in a whisper.

Does collagen actually do anything?

The 8g of marine collagen peptides in every bottle is the same beauty-grade form featured in your favorite skincare powders. We don't make medical claims — we just think it tastes incredible and feels like a soft-luxury habit.

How long does it stay fresh?

Each bottle is hand-finished and chilled to 2°C before it leaves us. Once it lands, keep it refrigerated and drink within 7 days for peak silk. No preservatives, no shortcuts.

Where do you ship?

Right now, NYC, LA, and Miami — 48-hour chilled delivery on every order. We're expanding to SF and Toronto this season. Drop your zip on the order page to check.

Is it sweet? Is there sugar?

Softly sweet, not dessert-sweet. We use a touch of pearl sugar and the natural sweetness of oat & almond milk. 9g of sugar per bottle, no syrups, no aftertaste.
